Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our painting services

How long has Arrow Elite Painters been in business?

+

Arrow Elite Painters has been serving the Tulsa metro area for over 15 years. We've built our reputation on quality workmanship, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

What areas do you serve?

+

We provide painting services throughout the Tulsa metropolitan area, including Tulsa, Broken Arrow, Bixby, Jenks, and surrounding communities.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Yes, Arrow Elite Painters is fully licensed as a painting contractor in Oklahoma and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage for your protection.

Do you offer free estimates?

+

Absolutely! We provide detailed, written estimates at no cost. Our estimates include a complete breakdown of materials, labor, and project timeline.

Do you guarantee your work?

+

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Interior painting comes with a 5-year warranty, and exterior painting includes a 10-year warranty against peeling and fading.

How much does interior painting cost?

+

Interior painting costs vary based on room size, paint quality, and project complexity. On average, expect $3-6 per square foot. We provide detailed estimates tailored to your specific project.

What affects the cost of exterior painting?

+

Exterior painting costs depend on home size, surface condition, paint quality, and accessibility. Factors like extensive prep work, multiple stories, or detailed trim work may increase costs.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

We accept various payment methods including cash, check, and credit cards. For larger projects, we can discuss payment schedules that work with your budget.

Are there any hidden fees?

+

No, we believe in transparent pricing. Our detailed estimates include all costs upfront. Any changes to the original scope will be discussed and approved before proceeding.

How long does a typical painting project take?

+

Timeline varies by project size and scope. Interior rooms typically take 1-3 days, whole house interiors 3-7 days, and exterior painting 3-10 days depending on size and weather conditions.

What preparation work do you do?

+

We handle all necessary prep work including cleaning, sanding, filling holes, caulking gaps, and priming surfaces. Proper preparation is essential for a long-lasting paint job.

Do I need to move my furniture?

+

We'll help move and protect your furniture. Small items should be removed, but we can move larger pieces to the center of the room and cover them with plastic sheeting.

What about cleanup?

+

Complete cleanup is included in every project. We remove all materials, clean up paint drips, and leave your space clean and ready to enjoy.

Can you work around my schedule?

+

We strive to accommodate your schedule and minimize disruption. For commercial projects, we offer after-hours and weekend work to avoid business interruption.

What brands of paint do you use?

+

We use premium quality paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and Behr. We'll recommend the best paint type for your specific project and budget.

Can I supply my own paint?

+

Yes, you can provide your own paint, though we recommend using our preferred suppliers for quality assurance. Keep in mind that warranty coverage may be affected by paint quality.

Do you use low-VOC paints?

+

Yes, we prioritize using low-VOC and eco-friendly paints, especially for interior projects. These paints have minimal odor and are safer for your family and the environment.

How do I choose the right paint finish?

+

Paint finish depends on the room and usage. We typically recommend eggshell or satin for living areas, semi-gloss for kitchens and bathrooms, and flat or eggshell for ceilings. We'll guide you through the best options.
